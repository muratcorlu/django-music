    Store your music library on internet

    django-music is an application for Django. With django-music app, you can upload all of your MP3 collection to your host and listen them from any device that has a browser. Never loose your library, access your library at home, at work or at mobile.

    Listen your music library from your PC, smartphone or tablet

    django-music player built with awsome jPlayer add-on. You can listen your library on every device that has a browser that has HTML5 audio or Flash support.

    Manage your library

    Make playlists, edit artist, genre or album informations and export your mp3 files to anywhere at anytime.
